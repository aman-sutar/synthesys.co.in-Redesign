A platform to automate and accelerate your admission process — from application submissions to final selections. Synthesys helps institutions go paperless, boost efficiency, and deliver a smooth enrollment experience for both administrators and students.

"Synthesys Online Admission System assures superior level of ease, reliability and efficiency in operations and has strongly emerged as a proven sought solution."

Synthesys’s Online Admission System has been developed as a fully featured cloud-based solution to empower the admission teams of various educational systems and simplify the admission process. It is intended for colleges, educational institutes, and universities advancing to automate their admission process by accepting enrollments online for various educational programs and courses offered and processing them till actual student admission. Online Admission System has been designed in accordance with the entire admission process starting from student information collection and management, accepting payments, document scrutiny either online or physically by scheduling an appointment, generating merit lists, interview scheduling, reporting and other related activities. The system has been designed considering all the process related aspects, norms and conditions. The solution carries an advantage to further get tailored as per organization-specific requirements.

Feature

Application Management

Systematic management of registration, tracking, submission and confirmation process along with consolidating candidate specific data.

Online Payment Acceptance

Enables the candidate to pay fees online towards the application in a highly secure environment. The payment can be made using almost all available payment gateways.

Documents Handling & Verification

Offers to let candidates submit the documents online. The later stages involve documents’ verification and organized storage.

Grievance Redressal System

Institutes receive and act on complaints reported by candidates enabling prompt actions on any issue raised by them and to let them avail services more effectively.

Merit List Generation

Automatic creation of merit lists based on the applications received for the courses institute offers. The merit list creation logic is provided by the institute at the time of initial configuration of the system.

Allotment Management

The seat allotment process enables the candidate to choose the institute as per preference and confirm the allotment in accordance with availability.
